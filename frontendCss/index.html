Who We Are

The CHEM Avengers are a group of California chemistry and math faculty funded to create student and faculty curriculum to support developing students' identity and self-perception as capable STEM thinkers and learners.

The CHEM Avengers span four partner institutions across California higher education: College of Marin, Diablo Valley College, Sonoma State University, and the University of California Berkeley with a shared vision that brilliance in science takes many forms. We are working to change the narrative in STEM education to create equity and build inclusion. Modules in this course have hands-on data-driven curricular materials to support student success in Introductory Chemistry courses.
